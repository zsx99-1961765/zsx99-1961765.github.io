Polo G - RAPSTAR

+

No.1

OnTrend

"Rapstar" (stylized in all caps) is a song by American rapper Polo G. It was released on April 9, 2021, by Columbia Records. The song was produced by Synco, with social media star Einer Bankz performing the ukulele. On the song, Polo G harmonizes about his growing stardom, the downside of fame and the rewards of it.

Boosie Badazz - Period

+

No.2

OnTrend

Boosie Badazz had a pretty eventful 2020. From a musical standpoint, the Baton Rouge legend blessed his fans with three projects — In House, a collaborative effort with his in-house producer Jit The Beast, Goat Talk 2, and Badass MO3, the last of which was a joint project with late Dallas rapper MO3.
